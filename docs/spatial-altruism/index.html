Research Basis

This implementation follows the steady-state variable-density Mitteldorf-Wilson model. Altruists pay cost c, distribute benefit b across a five-site von Neumann neighborhood, and each site is reoccupied through a local weighted lottery among altruist, selfish, and empty competitors.

In the code, harshness is the void fitness eta and disease is the extra void lottery mass xi. The repository also supports periodic uniform-culling and compact-swath disturbance variants, but this replay stays on the steady-state configuration.

Observable mechanics prove an equation-level match to the steady-state Mitteldorf-Wilson model shown here. They do not, by themselves, prove whether this Python implementation was historically derived directly from the paper or through an intermediate implementation.
