Model

Core Rule

Each site carries a continuous cooperation trait h and an inherited lineage label. Cooperation creates benefit b × h, pays private cost c × h, and splits the benefit into an open share for the full local neighborhood plus a retained share for same-lineage recipients only.

The replay therefore visualizes a direct test of the statement: cooperation rises when enough of its return flows back to cooperators or their copies.
